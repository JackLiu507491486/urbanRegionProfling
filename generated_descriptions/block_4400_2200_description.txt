The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, agricultural, scientific, and governmental functions. It contains a number of company enterprises including manufacturing (metal and hardware), construction engineering, trade, agricultural development, and landscaping companies. Supporting these functions are industrial facilities (factories), agricultural services (farm machinery), and research and development related to agriculture and water resources.

Several government and public service institutions are present, including a district-level water resources extension station, village committee, and public toilets. Two bus stops provide local transportation access. Limited retail services are provided by a convenience store and a local agricultural service station. A science and education research site is also located in the area. Finally, there is a recreational area related to agriculture. The layout of the area suggests a mixed-use development, with industrial, agricultural, research, and community functions co-existing.
