The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, residential, commercial, and recreational functions. Industrial activity is indicated by the presence of numerous companies, including those involved in machinery, electronics, manufacturing, and trading. Residential areas are represented by housing complexes and related services. Commercial activity is evident through various shopping options, including supermarkets, convenience stores, specialty shops, and seafood markets. The area also contains dining establishments, with a focus on seafood restaurants. Recreational and cultural elements are present in the form of a resort, a community recreation center, cultural squares, and educational institutions. Transportation infrastructure is supported by bus stops and parking facilities. Government and public service facilities are distributed throughout the area.
