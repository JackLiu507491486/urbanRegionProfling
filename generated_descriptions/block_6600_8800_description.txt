The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, agricultural, residential, and community service functions. It contains a significant number of companies and factories, particularly in the packaging, manufacturing, and automotive sectors. Agricultural activity is evident through cooperatives, farms, and related sales. Residential elements are indicated by community centers and a kindergarten. Local commerce is supported by supermarkets, convenience stores, and markets, including those specializing in agricultural products and construction materials. Transportation infrastructure is present, with numerous bus stops and parking facilities, as well as automotive service businesses such as repair shops, tire retailers, and charging stations. Leisure and health services include a nursing home, recreational farms, and local restaurants.
