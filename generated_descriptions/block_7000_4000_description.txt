The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, recreational, and agricultural land uses. A significant number of POIs are categorized as companies involved in manufacturing, technology, logistics, and trade. Recreational functions are represented by the presence of the Hanxiang Water Park, related parking facilities, and leisure farms. Agricultural activity is indicated by POIs related to agricultural production and farming culture. Public transportation infrastructure, including bus stops, and public facilities like restrooms are also present. The spatial layout suggests a relatively dispersed arrangement of industrial sites, recreational areas, and agricultural lands, supported by transportation infrastructure.
