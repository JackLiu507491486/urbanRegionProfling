The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, residential, and administrative functions. A significant number of POIs are categorized as company enterprises, including factories and various types of companies involved in manufacturing, technology, and trade. Public transportation is facilitated by numerous bus stops. The area also includes local government and community service facilities, such as village committees, a women's center, a Red Cross service station, and a volunteer work station. Retail activity is represented by convenience stores and shops. Support for transportation is present in the form of a gas station and a tire shop.
