The area is characterized by a mix of residential, commercial, agricultural, and recreational functions. Residential areas are represented by apartment complexes and residential communities. Commercial activity includes hotels, restaurants, retail stores (furniture, groceries, etc.), and various service providers (e.g., beauty salons, real estate agencies, car repair). Agricultural activity is indicated by the presence of agricultural cooperatives, farms, and produce markets. Recreational facilities include resorts, a family farm, and a grape-picking garden. The area also contains several company enterprises covering mechanical, trading, and other services, as well as governmental and community organizations. Transportation infrastructure includes parking lots and a gas station. Furthermore, educational institutions such as a primary school and kindergarten are present, alongside medical facilities including clinics and a community health center.
