The area is characterized by a mix of agricultural, industrial, educational, and recreational land uses. Agricultural presence is indicated by farms, nurseries, and companies related to agriculture and landscaping. Industrial activities are represented by companies involved in coatings, door/window manufacturing, and organic fertilizer production. Educational functions are evident through driving schools, training centers, and military summer camps. Recreational facilities include fitness areas and a fruit orchard. The area is served by a network of bus stops and contains a public restroom.
