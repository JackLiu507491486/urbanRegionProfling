The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, agricultural, and residential support services. The presence of numerous factories and companies indicates an industrial function, with a focus on manufacturing (e.g., metal products, plastics, machinery, textiles, and furniture). Agricultural activity is evidenced by the presence of a牧场(pasture) and a 生猪养殖专业合作社(pig breeding cooperative). Transportation infrastructure is represented by several bus stops along 奉干公路 and 平庄公路, and a 中国石油加油站. Public facilities include public restrooms, a village health clinic, village committee, and village Party branch. Limited retail services are available, including convenience stores and a supermarket.
