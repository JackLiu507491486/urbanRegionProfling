The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, commercial, and limited community services. A significant portion of the POIs are company enterprises, including companies, factories, and agricultural bases. These enterprises span various sectors, such as food production, packaging, wood products, concrete, and technology. Transportation is supported by a number of bus stops located throughout the area. Community services include a clinic, a village committee, convenience stores, and driving schools with associated testing facilities. There is also a limited presence of retail and dining options. The presence of industrial parks suggests a degree of planned development for industrial activity.
