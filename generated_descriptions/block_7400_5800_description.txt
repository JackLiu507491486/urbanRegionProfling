The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, residential, commercial, and public service functions. A significant portion of the POIs are company enterprises, including factories and businesses engaged in manufacturing and trading activities, particularly in the machinery and electronics sectors. Residential areas, such as apartment complexes, are also present. Commercial activity is indicated by shopping centers, supermarkets, convenience stores, furniture stores, restaurants and automotive services including gas stations. Public services include bus stations, parking lots, public toilets, a clinic, schools, village committees, and emergency shelters.
