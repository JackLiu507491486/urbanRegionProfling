The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, commercial, and residential functions. A significant portion of the POIs are company enterprises and factories, indicating a strong industrial presence with a variety of sectors including chemical, mechanical, electronic, and textile industries. Several industrial parks are also located within the area. Supporting these industrial activities, there are logistics and maintenance services. Commercial activities are represented by supermarkets, convenience stores, shopping centers, and restaurants. Transportation infrastructure is evident through the presence of bus stops and parking lots. Recreational facilities such as a fishing farm and internet cafe are also present.
