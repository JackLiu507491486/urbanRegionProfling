The area is characterized by a mix of residential, commercial, and light industrial functions. Residential areas are represented by numerous apartment complexes. Commercial activity is evident through a variety of restaurants, supermarkets, convenience stores, and service-oriented businesses, including automotive services, real estate agencies, and beauty salons. Light industrial presence is indicated by a car parts factory and advertising companies. Public services and governmental organizations are also located in the area, including community centers, government offices, and a toll collection station. Transportation infrastructure is represented by bus stops and both public and private parking lots.
