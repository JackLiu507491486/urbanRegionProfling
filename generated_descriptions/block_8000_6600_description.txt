The area is characterized by a mixed-use environment including industrial, residential, commercial, and service functions. A significant number of POIs are related to company enterprises, including factories and various types of companies involved in manufacturing, trade, and technology. Residential areas are indicated by the presence of numerous residential complexes. Commercial activity is reflected in a variety of shopping services such as supermarkets, convenience stores, and specialized shops, as well as catering services including restaurants. Public services and amenities are also present, as evidenced by healthcare facilities, schools, government agencies, transportation services (bus stops and parking lots), and public restrooms. The area exhibits a blend of industrial activity with residential and commercial services to support the local population.
