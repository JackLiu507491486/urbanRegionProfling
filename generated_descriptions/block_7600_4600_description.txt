The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, agricultural, residential, and limited commercial activity. A significant portion of the POIs are company enterprises, spanning diverse sectors including manufacturing, technology, pharmaceuticals, and logistics. Public transportation is supported by several bus stops. There are also a few recreational areas such as a blueberry picking garden and a senior activity center. Limited retail and dining options are present, with convenience stores and restaurants scattered throughout the area. Logistics services are present. A local government entity (village committee) is also located within the area.
