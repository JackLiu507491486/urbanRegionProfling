The area is characterized by a mix of residential, commercial, educational, and industrial functions. Residential areas are represented by numerous apartment complexes and residential districts. Commercial activity is evident through a variety of restaurants, supermarkets, convenience stores, home furnishing and building materials shops, and various specialized stores. Educational facilities are present, including Shanghai University of Political Science and Law and related educational institutions. Industrial activity is indicated by the presence of various companies and factories. The area is supported by transportation infrastructure, including bus stops, a long-distance bus station, and parking lots. Additional features include government and public facilities, recreational venues and accommodation services, indicating a comprehensive and mixed-use environment.
