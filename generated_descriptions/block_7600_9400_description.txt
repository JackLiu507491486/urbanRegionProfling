The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, agricultural, commercial, and recreational functions. Industrial activities are represented by factories, metal and machinery manufacturers, a container yard, and building material companies. Agricultural presence is indicated by a water production cooperative. Commercial activities include logistics services, building materials, and furniture sales. A flower-themed scenic spot with a flower exhibition hall and public restrooms are also present. The area also contains traffic-related infrastructure at the intersection of Bingguo Road and Lingang Avenue. The spatial layout suggests a mixed-use zone with industry, agriculture, commerce and recreation.
