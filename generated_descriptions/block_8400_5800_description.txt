The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, agricultural/recreational, and residential/community functions. Industrial activities are prominent, indicated by the presence of numerous factories, manufacturing companies, and industrial parks. Agricultural and recreational activities are represented by several farms and pick-your-own strawberry fields. Residential and community functions are supported by public transportation (bus stops), convenience stores, a clinic, elderly care facilities, village committees, and some residential buildings. The spatial layout appears to be distributed, with industrial areas interspersed with agricultural land and pockets of residential and community services. Transportation infrastructure, including bus stops and a gas station, serves to connect these different zones.
