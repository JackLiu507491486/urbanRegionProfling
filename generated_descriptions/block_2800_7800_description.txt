The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, commercial, and residential functions. It contains several large companies related to shipbuilding and electronics, including Shanghai Jiangnan Changxing Shipbuilding Co. and associated companies. The area also features a concentration of company enterprises, alongside an industrial park, suggesting an active industrial zone.

Commercial activity is evident through the presence of a four-star hotel (Shanghai Huanghao Hotel) with supporting amenities such as a KTV, cigar bar, and conference hall, as well as various retail outlets including supermarkets, convenience stores, and a mobile phone store. Automotive services are also present.

Transportation infrastructure is represented by multiple bus stops along Jiangnan Avenue and Xinggan Road, facilitating public transport within the area. The presence of a logistics station (Cainiao Yizhan) indicates local package delivery services.
