The area is characterized by a mix of educational, residential, industrial, and commercial functions. A significant portion of the POIs are related to transportation, including a subway station with multiple entrances, numerous bus stops, and a high volume of parking facilities, particularly serving the large presence of the Tongji University JiaDing Campus. This suggests a high demand for transportation options. The area also contains a variety of residential complexes, including apartments, villas, and student dormitories. In addition, a notable number of companies and factories are located in the area, indicating industrial activity. Commercial activities are represented by restaurants, shops, and supermarkets. These POIs are spatially distributed to support the needs of the university, residential areas, and industrial workforce.
