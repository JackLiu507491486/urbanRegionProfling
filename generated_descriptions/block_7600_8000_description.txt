The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, agricultural, residential support, and governmental functions. Industrial activities are represented by companies engaged in machinery manufacturing, display design, bamboo production, and fan production. Agricultural activities are indicated by a water product aquaculture cooperative. Residential support services include a community elderly activity room, a public toilet, a village clinic, and a comprehensive agricultural service station. Governmental functions are represented by village committees and party branches. Recreational activities are represented by a fishing center.
