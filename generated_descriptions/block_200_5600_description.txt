The area contains a mix of land uses including agricultural, industrial, residential, governmental, commercial, recreational, and transportation functions. Agricultural activities are represented by agricultural cooperatives. Industrial presence is indicated by companies involved in cable manufacturing, engineering, concrete production and road maintenance. Residential areas are present in the form of detached villas. Governmental function is served by a village committee. Commercial activities include a convenience store and a building material store. Recreational facilities include leisure venues, an elderly activity center and a strawberry farm. Public transportation is supported by several bus stops.
