The area contains a mix of industrial, commercial, public service, and recreational functions. Industrial activity is indicated by the presence of various factories and companies involved in manufacturing and trading, including paper products, packaging, ceramics, machinery, and general trade. Commercial activity is represented by a supermarket. Public services are present in the form of public restrooms and a district-level government water management facility. Recreational services are represented by a village-level elderly activity center. A port/dock facility is also present.
