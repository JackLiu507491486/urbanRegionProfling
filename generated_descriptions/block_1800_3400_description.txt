The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, agricultural/recreational, and commercial activities. A significant portion of the POIs are company enterprises, including factories and companies engaged in manufacturing (e.g., machinery, metal products, packaging), commercial trade, and technology. Agricultural and recreational POIs include fishing centers, cultural activity rooms, and fruit picking gardens. Commercial activities are represented by retail shops (convenience stores, supermarkets, building materials), restaurants, and auto-related services. Transportation infrastructure is present, indicated by multiple bus stops. The area also includes some research facilities. The spatial layout suggests a combination of industrial parks/factory areas interspersed with agricultural land and localized commercial services.
