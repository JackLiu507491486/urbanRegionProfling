The area contains a mix of residential, commercial, industrial, governmental, and transportation-related points of interest. Commercial activities are represented by supermarkets, convenience stores, markets (fruit, vegetable, and agricultural products), and retail shops. Industrial presence includes a shipbuilding factory. Government and community functions are indicated by village committees, a post office, and a branch of the postal savings bank. Transportation infrastructure is composed of bus stops and a port. Additionally, there are recreational facilities and dining establishments.
