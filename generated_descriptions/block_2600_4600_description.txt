The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, commercial, residential, and recreational functions. It features a high concentration of companies, factories, and warehouses, indicating significant industrial activity. Supporting this activity are numerous logistics and freight services. Commercial activity is reflected in the presence of various shops, supermarkets, and restaurants. Residential elements are evident in the presence of apartment buildings, community centers, and elderly activity rooms. Recreational aspects include fishing ponds and related facilities. Transportation infrastructure is supported by bus stops and numerous parking lots. Public facilities like public toilets are distributed throughout the area.
