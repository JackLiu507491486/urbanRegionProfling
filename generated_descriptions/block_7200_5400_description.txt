The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, commercial, residential, and public service functions. Evidenced by the presence of numerous companies, factories, and industrial parks, a significant portion of the area is dedicated to industrial and manufacturing activities. Concurrently, the area features commercial activity, including restaurants, supermarkets, convenience stores, and a commercial street. Residential areas, such as apartment complexes, are also present. Public services are represented by government offices, schools, medical facilities, public restrooms, and emergency shelters. Transportation infrastructure is evident through the high number of parking lots and gas stations. The area also includes recreational facilities, such as KTVs, gyms, and chess rooms. Logistics services are represented by numerous logistics companies.
