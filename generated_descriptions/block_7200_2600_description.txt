The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, commercial, residential and agricultural land uses. A significant number of POIs are related to company enterprises, including factories, companies specializing in mechanical and electronic products, metallurgy and chemical industries, and agricultural bases. The presence of industrial parks and logistical companies suggests a focus on manufacturing and distribution. Furthermore, the area contains various retail businesses such as supermarkets, convenience stores, and specialized shops. Transportation infrastructure is supported by bus stops and parking lots. The area also includes public services such as government institutions, healthcare facilities, schools and leisure places.
