The area is characterized by a mixed-use urban environment. Transportation infrastructure is well-developed, evidenced by numerous bus stops, public and private parking facilities, a port, and a subway station. The area contains a mix of residential and commercial functions, as indicated by the presence of residential complexes, a commercial plaza, hotels, and apartment buildings. A wide variety of companies including logistics, manufacturing, and warehousing indicate the presence of industrial activity in the area. Public services include community health centers, government administration offices, and public toilets. Commercial activity is diverse, including general merchandise stores, supermarkets, specialty shops, home improvement, and auto-related services. Food and beverage services range from fast food to restaurants, including many regional cuisines. Recreational facilities include fitness centers, sports venues, and entertainment venues. Educational facilities are also present, including schools, training centers, and cultural centers.
