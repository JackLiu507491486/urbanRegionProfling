The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, residential, commercial, educational, and transportation functions. The presence of numerous companies and factories, particularly in the mechanical, electrical, and chemical sectors, indicates a significant industrial component. This is further supported by the presence of industrial parks and company-specific parking facilities. Residential areas are evident through the presence of villas, residential districts, and apartment complexes. Commercial activity is represented by a variety of shops, supermarkets, convenience stores, and restaurants. Educational institutions like vocational colleges and research centers contribute to the area's function. Transportation is supported by bus stops along major roads and parking facilities. The area also includes a limited number of recreational facilities such as a strawberry picking garden and an internet cafe.
