The area contains a mix of industrial, commercial, residential, and agricultural functions. A significant portion of the POIs are company enterprises, including manufacturers and packaging companies. Commercial activity is represented by various shopping services such as supermarkets, furniture stores, appliance retailers, and smaller shops. Accommodation services are present in the form of hotels and guesthouses. Several bus stops suggest public transportation access. Some recreational and agricultural activity is indicated by the presence of strawberry picking gardens. Basic services such as gas stations, pharmacies, auto repair shops, and beauty salons are also present.
