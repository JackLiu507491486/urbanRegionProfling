The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, agricultural, residential, commercial, and community service functions. Industrial activities are represented by factories and metal product companies. Agricultural presence is indicated by fruit and vegetable cooperatives and a cherry picking orchard. Residential functions are supported by a business/residential complex. Commercial activity is manifested through various retail outlets, including supermarkets, convenience stores, and home improvement stores. Community services are provided by a medical clinic, a village committee, and a church. Transportation infrastructure includes multiple bus stops. Logistics and warehousing are also present, indicated by storage facilities and freight forwarding services.
