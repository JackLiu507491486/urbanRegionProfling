The area contains a mix of residential, commercial, industrial, and administrative functions. A significant portion of the POIs are retail businesses, including convenience stores, supermarkets, and specialized shops for construction materials, furniture, clothing, and agricultural supplies. Several companies and factories are present, along with logistical services. Public services are represented by a community health center, a police station, government administration offices, schools (primary and secondary), post offices, and banks. Transportation infrastructure includes public parking and bus stops. There are also a limited number of recreational facilities, including a resort.
