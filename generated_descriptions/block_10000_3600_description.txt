The area is characterized by a concentration of industrial and logistical activities. The predominant land use is industrial, with a significant number of companies engaged in chemical manufacturing, refining, and related industries. This is evidenced by the presence of numerous companies categorized as "冶金化工" (metallurgical and chemical industry). There is also a notable presence of logistics and warehousing services, as indicated by the numerous "物流速递" (logistics and express delivery) POIs. Additional functions include vehicle services such as inspection and driver training. Limited commercial services such as restaurants and convenience stores are also present. The area appears to support the needs of the dominant industrial activities.
