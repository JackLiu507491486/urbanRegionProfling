The area contains a mixture of industrial, commercial, medical, and agricultural functions. Industrial activity is indicated by the presence of factories and various companies engaged in manufacturing, trading, and material distribution. Commercial activity is evident through convenience stores, a department store, a building materials store, and logistics services. A significant medical facility, the Jinshan Hospital affiliated with Fudan University, along with its associated parking, suggests a healthcare focus. Finally, a farming and breeding company indicates some level of agricultural presence. The POIs are spatially distributed to support the needs of residents, workers, and patients in the area.
