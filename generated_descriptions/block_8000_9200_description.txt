The area is characterized by a mix of educational, residential, commercial, and industrial functions. Educational facilities, including Shanghai Jianqiao College, Shanghai Dianji University, and affiliated vocational schools, are prominent. Supporting these institutions are student dormitories, sports facilities (gymnasiums), libraries, and associated service centers. Commercial activity is evident through convenience stores, a food plaza, and various restaurants. Industrial presence is indicated by companies such as Siemens Wind Power and Shanghai Construction Engineering Group. Transportation infrastructure includes public bus stops and parking facilities.
