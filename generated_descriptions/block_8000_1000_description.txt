The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, commercial, and service-oriented functions. Industrial activity is indicated by the presence of numerous factories and companies, including those involved in textiles, plastics, brewing, machinery, and medical equipment. Commercial activity is evident through a variety of retail establishments, such as clothing stores, supermarkets, specialty shops (e.g., tea, crafts, antiques), and convenience stores. Food and beverage services are also prevalent, with a mix of restaurants, bakeries, and specialty food shops. Additionally, there are several service-oriented POIs including telecommunication providers, real estate agencies, and personal care services. The area also includes a government agency and community service center.
