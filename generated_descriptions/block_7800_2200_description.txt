The area is characterized by a mix of residential, commercial, industrial, and institutional functions. Residential areas are represented by numerous apartment complexes. Commercial activity is indicated by a variety of retail stores (supermarkets, convenience stores, specialty shops), restaurants, and service providers (auto repair, beauty salons, real estate agencies). Industrial presence is suggested by the number of companies and factories. Institutional functions are fulfilled by schools (primary, secondary, and vocational), government agencies, community health services, and agricultural research facilities. Transportation infrastructure includes bus stops and parking lots. Recreational facilities such as parks, fishing spots, and sports clubs are also present.
