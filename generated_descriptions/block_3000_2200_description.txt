The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, residential, commercial, and community service functions. Industrial activity is represented by a large number of companies and factories, including those involved in manufacturing, processing, and agriculture. Residential areas are present, indicated by the presence of residential compounds. Commercial activity is evidenced by a variety of shops, supermarkets, and markets offering diverse goods and services. Community services include healthcare facilities, schools, government offices, recreational venues, and elder care facilities. The spatial layout consists of industrial parks interspersed with residential areas and commercial strips along main roads, supported by transportation infrastructure such as parking lots and bus stops.
