The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, agricultural, residential, and commercial activities. A significant number of POIs are related to company enterprises, particularly those involved in furniture manufacturing, wood processing, and decoration materials. Several factories and industrial companies are present. Agricultural activities are represented by fruit orchards and agricultural supply stores. Residential aspects are indicated by bus stops, elderly activity centers, and a rural clinic. Commercial activity is observed with a logistics center, a supermarket, and several retail outlets for furniture and agricultural supplies. The presence of an industrial park further supports the area's industrial function.
