The area is characterized by a mix of agricultural tourism, industrial activity, and residential/community services. A significant portion of the POIs are related to the "Nanhui Honey Peach" industry, including orchards, picking gardens, farmhouses, and related commercial activities (e.g., sales, tourism). Industrial activities are represented by various factories and companies involved in manufacturing and processing (e.g., metal products, machinery, plastics). The area also includes community facilities such as village committees, cultural centers, and public transportation (bus stops). Limited commercial services are present in the form of convenience stores and specialty shops.
