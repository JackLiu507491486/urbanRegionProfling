The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, commercial, residential, educational, and transportation functions. A significant number of company enterprises and factories are present, indicating a strong industrial presence. This is further supported by the presence of industrial parks. Transportation infrastructure is well-developed, featuring a subway station with multiple entrances and numerous bus stops. A high volume of parking facilities, including dedicated, public, and roadside parking, are available. Residential functions are present in the form of apartment complexes and villas. Educational facilities include schools ranging from elementary to high school levels. Commercial activities are represented by logistics companies, automobile sales and repair shops, convenience stores, and financial institutions. Dining options are primarily workplace cafeterias.
