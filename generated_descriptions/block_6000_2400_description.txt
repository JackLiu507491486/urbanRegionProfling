The area is characterized by a mix of recreational, residential, and commercial functions centered around the Shanghai Chenshan Botanical Garden. The area includes the Shanghai Chenshan Botanical Garden and related attractions. A significant number of parking facilities and bus stops support transportation to and from the botanical garden. Additionally, a number of accommodation options, including hotels, villas, and guesthouses, are present, some of which are specifically marketed as "party villas." The area also contains some corporate and agricultural entities.
