The area is characterized by a mixed-use environment. A significant portion of the area is dedicated to industrial and manufacturing activities, as evidenced by the numerous factories and companies related to metal products, textiles, and industrial materials. The presence of several automotive service businesses (repair, sales, and maintenance) indicates a focus on vehicle-related services. Commercial activity is also prevalent, with a variety of retail stores, supermarkets, and home furnishing/building material markets. Additionally, there are a number of dining establishments and lodging options. The area includes some agricultural/recreational aspects with the presence of orchards and picking gardens. Finally, the area is serviced by public transportation with multiple bus stops along with transportation infrastructure elements.
