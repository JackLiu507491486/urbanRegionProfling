The area is characterized by a mix of residential, commercial, industrial, and transportation functions. Residential presence is indicated by numerous apartment complexes, residential communities, and villas. Commercial activity is reflected in the presence of restaurants, supermarkets, pharmacies, various retail stores, and sales offices. Industrial activity is shown by the presence of many companies. Transportation infrastructure is well-developed, featuring a subway station with multiple entrances/exits, several bus stops, and parking lots. Some recreational facilities and accommodation services also exist.
