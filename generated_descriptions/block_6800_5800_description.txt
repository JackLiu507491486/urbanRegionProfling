The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, commercial, agricultural, and residential functions. A significant portion of the POIs are company enterprises, including factories, packaging companies, trading companies, and agricultural bases. Commercial activity is evident through the presence of numerous convenience stores, supermarkets, and fruit wholesale markets, including a concentration of fruit wholesale businesses. Additionally, there are transportation facilities such as bus stops and parking lots. The area also includes public facilities like public toilets, elderly care facilities, and a hotel, along with government and community organizations.
