The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, agricultural, recreational, and logistical functions. A significant number of POIs are categorized as companies or factories, indicating an industrial presence. These include companies involved in shipbuilding, machinery, metalworking, packaging, and logistics. Agricultural activities are represented by farms, orchards (strawberry picking), and agricultural cooperatives. Recreational facilities such as equestrian clubs and farms are also present. Transportation infrastructure is notable, including a port/dock, several bus stops and logistical services. Commercial activities are supported by markets, convenience stores and car dealerships. There are also some residential and community support services such as kindergartens, clinics and bathhouses.
