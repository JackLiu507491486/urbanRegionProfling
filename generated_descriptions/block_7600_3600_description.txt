The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, residential, commercial, and logistical functions. The predominant land use is industrial, evidenced by the high concentration of companies, factories, and industrial parks spanning various sectors, including machinery, electronics, metal products, plastics, and chemical manufacturing. Supporting this industrial activity are logistics services, including multiple courier and logistics companies. Residential areas are present in the form of residential complexes and apartments. Commercial activity is represented by supermarkets, convenience stores, and specialized shops. Transportation infrastructure is indicated by the presence of multiple bus stops. Limited healthcare services are available, as indicated by a single clinic. A research facility is also present.
