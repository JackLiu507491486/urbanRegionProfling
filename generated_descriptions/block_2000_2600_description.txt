The area is characterized by a mix of industrial, logistical, governmental, and transportation-related functions. The dominant land use appears to be industrial, indicated by the high number of company and factory POIs, encompassing diverse sectors like automotive parts, machinery, chemicals, and cosmetics. Supporting this industrial activity are numerous transportation facilities, including bus stops distributed along major roads and a high concentration of parking lots, primarily roadside parking. The area also contains several industrial parks. Governmental and community services are present, including village committees, a judicial services base, and a cultural center. Limited retail presence is observed, consisting mainly of convenience stores and shopping-related services. A single sports facility (racetrack) is present.
